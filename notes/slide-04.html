Problem Agitators — Slide 4

Beat: Three separate reasons why doing nothing gets worse, not better.

Say: "Card one — every new subscriber to your newsletter lands on a platform you don't own. If LinkedIn changes the algorithm or your account has an issue, you can lose access overnight. Card two — without a system, moving those subscribers off LinkedIn yourself means hours of manual copy-paste work, which is exactly the kind of thing you told me isn't your strength. Card three — every week this sits, more of your audience exists only on a platform you don't control."

Watch out: Don't hint at the solution here. Let the pain land. The promise comes on the next slide. Keep this about the newsletter specifically — not her book or app.
