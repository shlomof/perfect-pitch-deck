Problem Agitators — Slide 4

Beat: Three separate reasons why doing nothing gets worse, not better.

Say: "Card one — your list keeps growing, from the newsletter, LinkedIn, and soon the book — but every new name lands with zero profile data. Card two — your best outbound is still manual. You said it yourself: scanning LinkedIn for who got promoted or mentioned a challenge. That doesn't scale as the list grows. Card three — warm signals going cold. Every newsletter and LinkedIn post you send generates goodwill and intent but leaves no qualification data behind. The people closest to hiring you are cooling every week you wait."

Watch out: Don't hint at the solution here. Let the pain land. The promise comes on the next slide. Don't make this only about the book — it's every channel he already has.
