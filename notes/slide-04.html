Problem Agitators — Slide 4

Beat: Three separate reasons why doing nothing gets worse, not better.

Say: "Card one — the book is coming. It's going to bring new readers into your list every week. Without a system live first, every one of them arrives without a profile. You'll never know who among them is a consulting prospect. Card two — your best outbound is still manual. You said it yourself: scanning LinkedIn for who got promoted or mentioned a challenge. That doesn't scale once the book amplifies your visibility. Card three — warm signals going cold. Every newsletter you send is generating goodwill and intent but leaving no qualification data behind. The people closest to hiring you are cooling every week you wait."

Watch out: Don't hint at the solution here. Let the pain land. The promise comes on the next slide.
