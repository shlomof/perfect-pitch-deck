What's Included — Slide 18

Beat: Four concrete deliverables, quickly — this is the "what am I actually getting" slide.

Say: "Concretely: works with however you already collect interest, a personalized nurture sequence, a dynamic site that matches each visitor, and tagging so you know what someone's interested in before you ever call them."

Scenario to use live: "If someone's looked at the Premium tier three times, that's tagged differently than someone who glanced at Land once. By the time you call them, you already know which conversation you're walking into."

Watch out: Keep it to these four — don't pad with generic inclusions like "support" or "consultation" that every vendor claims. Card 1 is "Setup," not "Connect" or "Capture" — don't claim to plug into a specific existing form/system (event sign-up and website contact form may not even be the same system) or name a specific tool like Google Form — we haven't confirmed either with TID.
