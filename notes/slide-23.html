If Peter has his own copy of the deck open: You cannot see what he clicks. Don't wait for him to click something. Say: "I want to check in — what's your gut telling you right now?" Then listen. His answer tells you which path you're on.

He clicks / says "I'm ready": The PayPal link opens for the $4,500 setup fee. Say: "Perfect. Once that goes through, I'll email you within 24 hours to book the kickoff call. Your list profiled and top prospects identified within 6 weeks — that's the commitment."

He clicks / says "I have a question": Say: "Great — ask me anything. What's on your mind right now?" Address it live on the call.

He clicks / says "Not right now": Don't push. Say: "Completely fair. What would need to change for this to make sense for you?" Listen. There's usually one specific thing. Address it if you can.
